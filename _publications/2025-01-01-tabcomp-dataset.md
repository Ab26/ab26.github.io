This paper introduces TabComp, a dataset for visual table reading comprehension. The dataset is designed to advance research in understanding and extracting information from tables in documents. The work contributes to the field of Natural Language Processing by providing resources for developing and evaluating models that can comprehend tabular data in a visual context.
